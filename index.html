1. Butterchicken


Contents:

  • 500 gms chicken breast
  • 1 cup curd
  • 1 tbsp salt(to taste)
  • 1 tbsp chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tbsp garlic
  • 1 tsp tandoori colour
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • Time: 120 minutes / 2 hours
  • Serves: 8

Step 1: Mix all together. Marinate for half an hour / 1 hour.
Step 2: Cook in it's own marination without adding water or oil. Cook for 8 to 10 minutes.
Step 3: Put on a skewer and burn on a normal gas till grilled.
Step 4: Enjoy wonderful butterchicken

2. Scrambled Egg

  • An egg
  • Masalas(optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil (depending on the size of your pan)

Step 1: Beat the egg in a bowl.
Step 2: Add salt and masalas(if you want to put masalas).
Step 3: Beat it again very strongly, but do not spill any of the mixture
Step 4: Grease a large pan with oil.
Step 5: Pour the egg in the center of the pan.
Step 6: Let it settle for about 2 minutes on a low flame.
Step 7: Scramble it in the pan, but be careful not to touch the metal side of the pan.
Step 8: Let it cook for a while on low flame.
Step 9: Quickly, but carefully, empty the egg in a plate/bowl
Step 10: Your scrambled egg is ready.
Tip: You can garnish your scrambled egg with chives, grated cheese and mint leaves.

3. Chocolate Cake

Contents

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder (preferably Dutch-processed)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda (not baking powder)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt(to taste)
  • 1 cup cool water
  • 6 Tablespoons vegetable oil (I use avocado oil)
  • 1 Tablespoon fresh lemon juice or distilled white vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla

    • Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease a 9 inch round cake pan.
    Step 2: Sift dry ingredients into a bowl and set aside.
    Step 3: In a measuring cup, combine water, oil, juice/vinegar, and vanilla. Step 4: Add liquids to bowl & gently stir until there are no lumps (about 30 seconds).
    Step 5: Pour into pan and bake for 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
    Step 6: Cool completely in the pan and frost, glaze, or dust with powdered sugar.
    Step 7: Enjoy your super chocolate cake!
